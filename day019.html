Nandhini's Chapatti Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm water
  • ¼ teaspoon ajwain seeds
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup butter or ghee
  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

For the filling:

  • ¼ kg of minced meat
  • 1 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 2 tomatoes finely chopped
  • ½ tablespoon garam masala
  • ¼ teaspoon red chilli powder
  • ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder

Instructions

  1. Mix the ajwain seeds and salt with flour.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture. Then add egg
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 5 minuites.
  5. Transfer the dough into a bowl and cover with wet cloth. Leave it for 30 minutes.
  6. After 30 minutes, roll it out into a large round shape.
  7. Heat it for 20 seconds one by one.
  8. Heat the butter for the filling and add onion, ginger garlic paste, tomatoes one by one. Saute for 4 minutes.
  9. Add masala powders. Mix thoroughly.
  10. Then add minced meat and salt. Cook it for 20 minutes or until oil is seperated.
  11. Cool it for 10 minutes.
  12. Spread the filling onto the chapatti then roll the chapatti into a swiss roll.
  13. Cut the roll into half and place flat into a baking tray.
  14. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rolls for 5 - 7 minutes.
